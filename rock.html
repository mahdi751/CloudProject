What is Rock?

The sound of rock is traditionally centered on the amplified electric guitar, which emerged in its modern form in the 1950s with the popularity of rock and roll. Also, it was influenced by the sounds of electric blues guitarists.

What styles do we have in rock ?

Rock 'n' Roll

Rock 'n' roll is a popular music genre that combines elements of rhythm and blues (R&B), jazz, and country music with the addition of electric instruments.

Indie Rock

Alternative rock had its breakthrough in the 80s as an alternative to rock. Alternative rock is defined by edgy lyrics and experimental use of instrumentation.

Blues Rock

As its name implies, blues rock is a combination of blues and rock music. Blues rock pieces typically have a loud beat, aggressive texture, heavy guitar sounds, and blues-scale guitar solos.

Alternative Rock

Alternative rock had its breakthrough in the 80s as an alternative to rock. Alternative rock is defined by edgy lyrics and experimental use of instrumentation.

What made rock so popular, and when ?

It is certainly arguable that by the end of the 20th century rock was the world's dominant form of popular music. Originating in the United States in the 1950s. People love rock music because it provided an alternative root of escape into the world of songs and music rather than those old antiquated styles.
