What is Hiphop?

hip hop music consists of rhythmic lyrics making use of techniques like assonance, alliteration, and rhyme. The rapper is accompanied by an instrumental track, usually referred to as a " beat," performed by a DJ.

What styles do we have in hiphop ?

Old school

Old-school hip hop is the earliest commercially recorded hip hop music and original style of the genre.It is characterizedby the simpler rapping techniques of the time and the general focus on party-relatedsubject matter.

Boom-bap

The style is usually recognized by a main drum loop that uses a hard-hitting, acoustic bass drum sample on the downbeats.

Country rap

Country rap is a style of music that combines hip-hop vocals with the harmonic language and song structure of country music.

Drill

The lyrics of drill tend to be violent and very gritty.Drill lyrics typically reflect life on the streets, and tend to be gritty, violent, realistic and nihilistic.The beat tends to be of a slow tempo - usually 60 or 70 beats per minute.

What made hiphop so popular ?

The rhyming created by rappers is considered by many to be o­ne of the most sophisticated styles of poetry. it is more than just a genre, it is a culture that has influenced America since the 1970's and gained a big popularity around the world.
